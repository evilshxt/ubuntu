My Story

I'm Washington, a dedicated Computer Science student at UMaT, currently in my third year. My journey here began with a simple belief: every student deserves a voice, and every voice deserves to be heard.

From my first day on campus, I've been actively involved in student life, serving as a department representative and later as a committee member. I've witnessed firsthand the challenges we face as students – from inadequate WiFi infrastructure to limited recreational facilities.

"True leadership isn't about holding titles; it's about lifting others up and creating lasting change that benefits everyone."

My campaign for SRC President is built on the foundation of Ubuntu – "I am because we are." Together, we can transform UMaT into the premier institution we know it can be.
